Spring offers an excellent set of tools for implementing backend applications. It makes your life easier with the different functionalities you generally implement in a backend solution, from integration with other apps to persistence in various database technologies. It's not wonder developers often use Spring for such applications. The framework basically offers you everything you need in such implementations and is an excellent fit for any kind of architectural style. 

The users interact with the client apps to manage their data 
Other backend solutions make direct to your backend app
Your backend app directly communicates with other backend solutions 
The client app make requests to your backend app to resolve user's request 
Your backend app uses a message broker and adds messages in queue or topic 


A backend app interacts in several ways with other apps and uses databases to manage data. Usually, backend apps is complex and may require the uses of various technologies. Frameworks simplify the implementation by providing tools you can use to implement the backend solution faster.
